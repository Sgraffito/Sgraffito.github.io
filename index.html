Help

Drawing in Gigil Faces

How do I start a new drawing?

There are two ways to start a new drawing in Gigil Faces:

  1. On the home page of the Gigil Faces app, tap on "New Drawing." You will be taken to a new screen called the Drawing Board.

  2. If you are in the Drawing Board and want to start a new drawing, you can tap the settings button on the lower right hand part of the screen and then choose "Make new drawing." Your old drawing will be saved and a new piece of paper will be laid out for you to draw on.

How do I change the brush color?

The toolbar on the right side of the screen contains 14 colors. The currently selected color is displayed in the selected color box, the big square on top of the right toolbar. To change the color, simply tap the color you want to use. The color in the color selected box should change to the color you selected.

How do I change the brush size?

Tap on the resize brush button in the left toolbar. When the pop-over appears, drag the slider to resize the brush.

How do I change the brush transparency?

Tap on the change brush transparency button in the left toolbar. When the pop-over appears, drag the slider to change the brush transparency.

How do I undo a drawing mistake?

To undo a drawing mistake, click on the undo button on the bottom of the screen.You can only undo the past seven marks you made with a brush.

How do I redo a part of a drawing that I erased with the undo button?

To redo a part of the drawing that was removed by the undo button, tap on the redo button. You can only redo the last seven marks that you removed with the undo button.

Animations and Stickers in Gigil Faces

How do I add an animated image to my drawing?

To add an animated image to the drawing board, tap the add animations button, and then tap once on the image you want to add from the pop-over. To view the animation, click on the play button on the bottom of the screen.

How do I add a sticker to my drawing?

To add a sticker to the drawing board, tap the add sticker button, and then tap on the sticker you want to add from the pop-over. Stickers will not animate when you press the play button.

How do I move, resize, and rotate animated images and stickers?

Once the image has been added to the drawing board, tap on the image once to select it: a red circle with an "x" in the middle will appear in the upper right hand corner of the image. Use two fingers to resize, move, or rotate the image. You will not be able to draw when an image is selected. Tap on the image once more to get rid of the red circle.

How do I delete animated images and stickers?

Tap the image once to select it: you will see a red circle with an x in the middle in the upper right hand corner of the image. Tap on the red circle to delete the image.

How do I animate my drawing?

First, make sure you have added some animated images to your drawing. Next, click on the play button on the bottom of the screen. Your image will start to animate. Press the pause button to stop the animation.
